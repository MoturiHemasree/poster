Luca

Luca is a coming-of-age story about a boy and his newfound best friend experiencing an unforgettable summer filled with gelato, pasta and endless scooter rides. The movie is set in a beautiful seaside town on the Italian Riviera. However, their fun is threatened by a secret, they are sea monsters from another world. Pixar's Luca offers the studio's hallmark visual splendor, yet fails to venture outside of safe waters.
